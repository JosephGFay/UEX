Single Day Adventure

Morning Activity

Relax on the white, sandy beaches of Yellow Leaf Bay or explore Taniti City’s unique native architecture.

Afternoon Activitiy

Enjoy lunch at a local Taniti City restaurant, then take a boat or bus tour, hike in the rainforest, or visit Taniti’s active volcano.

Night Activity

Experience Taniti's nightlife by visiting local pubs, dancing at the new dance club, or enjoying games at the arcade in Merriton Landing.
