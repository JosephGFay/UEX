Taniti

Island Time

Taniti, a small tropical island in the pacific.

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, most the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture.

The island offers a range of restaurants, lodging options, and entertainment activities such as snorkeling, zip-lining, and museum visits, with many attractions centered around Taniti City and Merriton Landing. Transportation includes a small airport (with expansion plans), buses, taxis, rental cars, and bikes, with Taniti City being especially walkable. Sightseeing highlights include beaches, rainforest hikes, and volcano tours.
